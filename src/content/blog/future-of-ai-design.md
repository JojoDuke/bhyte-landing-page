Artificial Intelligence has moved beyond simple chat interfaces. Today, AI is an active partner in the design process, helping us create more personalized, efficient, and stunning products.

## Why AI Design Matters

In the coming years, we'll see products that adapt in real-time to user behavior. Design won't just be static layouts; it will be a living, breathing ecosystem controlled by smart algorithms.

### Key Trends for 2026

1. **Behavioral Adaptivity**: Dynamic interfaces that change based on user mood and intent.
2. **Generative Layouts**: AI-generated components that optimize for conversion and accessibility automatically.
3. **Voice & Intent**: Moving beyond screens into seamless ambient interaction.
